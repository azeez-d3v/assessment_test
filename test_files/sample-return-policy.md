# Company Return Policy

## Overview

Our company is committed to customer satisfaction. We offer a comprehensive return policy to ensure you're happy with your purchase.

## Return Window

- **Standard Items:** 30 days from purchase date
- **Electronics:** 15 days from purchase date
- **Seasonal Items:** 7 days from purchase date

## Conditions for Return

1. Items must be in original packaging
2. Item must be unused and in original condition
3. Receipt or proof of purchase is required
4. Items must have all original tags attached

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Downloadable software
- Gift cards
- Intimate apparel
- Perishable goods

## Refund Process

1. Initiate return through our website or customer service
2. Ship item back using provided label
3. Refund processed within 5-7 business days
4. Original payment method will be credited

## Exchange Policy

Prefer an exchange? You can swap for a different size, color, or similar item at no extra cost. Just select "Exchange" when initiating your return.

## Contact Us

Questions about returns? Contact our support team at support@example.com or call 1-800-RETURNS.
